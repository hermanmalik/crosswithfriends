Cross with Friends terms & privacy

Last updated: 12/26/2017

What information do we collect?

When you sign in with facebook, we store a facebook-generated user id to keep track of your identity. This allows you to enjoy Cross with Friends on different devices and enjoy the same experience.

What do we do with this information?

Cross with Friends never supplies your personal information to any third party for advertising or promotional purposes.

Is my information secure?

At its current state, Cross with Friends cannot offer any guarantees with regards to security. We advise against transmitting any sensitive or personal data through the website.
